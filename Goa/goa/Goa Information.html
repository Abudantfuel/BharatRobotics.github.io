WELCOME TO GOA

Goa a Tiny Emerald Shaped State lanlocked by the States of Karnatka and Maharashtra is one of the most lively and colorful state of India.

Konkani is the traditional language of Goa.

The Popular Dances in Goa include Fugdi,Dhalo and Kunbi.The People of Goa Celebrate Several Festivals Throughout The Year,Some of Which Include-Goa Carnival,Sao Joao Festival,Goa Sunburn Festival,Christmas,Diwali

Best Time to Visit Goa is from Mid November to Mid Feburary
